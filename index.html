FAQ

Questions, Answered.

Do I need any experience to start?
Absolutely not. Most of our students walked in with zero martial arts background. Our fundamentals classes are designed specifically for beginners, and our community is famously welcoming to first-timers.
What should I wear and bring to my first class?
Comfortable workout clothes with no zippers, buttons, or jewelry. A water bottle and a positive attitude. We provide mats and any specialty equipment. If you don't own a gi yet, you can borrow one for your trial.
Is there a contract or long-term commitment?
Never. All memberships are month-to-month. Cancel any time, no fees, no questions. We'd rather earn your business every month than lock you in.
How young can kids start BJJ?
Our kids program welcomes young athletes ready to listen, learn, and have fun on the mats. Reach out and we'll help you figure out the right starting point for your child.
What's included with the recovery amenities?
All members get access to our infrared sauna, red light therapy, and recovery space. Premium tools that most gyms charge extra for — included in your membership.
Is BJJ safe? I'm worried about getting hurt.
BJJ is one of the safest combat sports because we drill controlled techniques and tap to submissions instead of taking damage. Beginners train with experienced partners who know how to move safely. Injuries happen far less than in contact sports like football or basketball.
