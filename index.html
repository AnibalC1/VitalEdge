Our Story

Built by Fighters.
For Everyone.

We didn't build Vital Edge to be another fitness center. We built it to be the place that transforms people.

What started as a passion for martial arts and performance became a mission: create a space in Millbury, MA where anyone — regardless of age, background, or fitness level — could access world-class coaching, elite equipment, and a community that holds you accountable.

Our coaches don't just teach — they train. They compete. They live what they teach. And they genuinely care whether you hit your goals.
