Questions

Frequently Asked

Is there a contract or commitment?+
No contracts, ever. Both plans are month-to-month and you can cancel at any time. We believe if we're doing our job, you'll want to stay.
How does the free trial work?+
Fill out the free trial form and we'll reach out to schedule your first session. No credit card required. Come train with us, see if it's a fit, and decide from there.
I'm a complete beginner — is that okay?+
Absolutely. Most of our members started with zero martial arts experience. We have beginner-specific classes and our instructors are known for being patient and welcoming. Everyone starts somewhere.
Can I switch plans later?+
Yes. You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Just talk to Pete or reach out via email and we'll take care of it.
What does the Basic plan include exactly?+
Basic gives you full access to the gym floor, open mat time, infrared sauna, red light therapy, and all recovery amenities. It does not include scheduled classes. It's perfect for members who want to self-direct their training or primarily use the recovery amenities.
Is the Full Access plan good for families?+
Yes — Full Access includes kids programs. Contact us for family plan details if you have multiple children you'd like to enroll.
